Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is simple (and will stay that way). If we make any changes, we will let you know through our Twitter feed and Discord channel. As this web site and app are open source (released under a Creative Commons Attribution License), you can also see a history of all changes with the differences highlighted so that you can quickly see what changed and when.

In general

We do not track you. We try our best to minimise the data we gather. We use self-hosted free and open tools whenever possible and we do not share your data with third parties.

Videos

Videos are hosted by Twitch, and served using the Twitch video player.

Analytics

The only analytics data we have are the anonymized download stats for Seepango, provided by the Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard.
